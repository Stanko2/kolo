Join Us in Reducing Waste and Supporting a Sustainable Bratislava!

We’re reaching out to nonprofit organizations like yours to invite you to be part of KOLO — Bratislava's first reuse center. Operated by OLO, KOLO is dedicated to promoting sustainable living by encouraging the reuse and repurposing of everyday items, helping to reduce waste and build a circular economy right here in our city.

Why Partner with Us?

  • Access affordable, quality items for your office or community spaces.
  • Reduce your environmental impact and promote a sustainable lifestyle.
  • Connect with like-minded organizations in our local movement.

Explore our current offerings or let us know what items you may need. We’d love to support your work with resources that align with your mission.
